Creating a collective product data network with purpose

Our vision is to create sustainable collective product data network that empowers all actors in realising new opportunities, innovating, enabling diversification and responsible choices. Solution: collective cooperation enabled by technology

Cooperation enabled by technology

We are building a cooperative dynamic network empowered by technology that:

  • Collects all type of product data from all sorts of touch points and systems
  • captures the meaning and context of the product data
  • translates it to collective data ontology
  • that allows to map it to local ontologies of any receiving points or systems
  • and distribute it in controlled and dynamic manners

The network allows ontology to grow and transform organically giving it unlimited potential of adaptation in various use cases from internal data management, data analysis, market expansion or product innovation.

Case: Popo

Project: Supplying ERP System with Product Data & Integrating Product Configurator

We collaborate with POPO's suppliers to collect, interpret, structure, and ensure the quality of product data before syncing it with the new ERP system, Odoo. This process encourages suppliers to internally reorganize their data and set up processes for recurring updates across all systems and external partners. Establishing a seamless flow of configurable product data to the ERP allows for improved data management and simplifies the ordering process.

Our goal is to build a robust digital data network with POPO’s suppliers, ensuring a continuous stream of reliable high-quality product data with minimal effort required for ongoing maintenance.
